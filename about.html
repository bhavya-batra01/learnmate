About LearnMate

LearnMate is a platform designed to help students connect with like-minded peers for collaborative learning. Whether you want to prepare for exams, discuss assignments, or join study sessions, LearnMate makes it easy and fun.

  • Find study buddies that match your interests and courses
  • Plan and schedule study sessions easily
  • Track your progress and achieve academic goals together
  • Chat and share resources in a safe and friendly environment
