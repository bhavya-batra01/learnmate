📘 Terms & Conditions for a Study Mate

1️⃣ Purpose

The study mate arrangement is meant for mutual learning, motivation, and academic support, not for copying or cheating.

2️⃣ Study Commitment

Both study mates should follow the decided study schedule. Regular participation is expected. Inform in advance if unable to attend a session.

3️⃣ Respect & Behavior

Be respectful, polite, and supportive. No bullying, disrespect, or negative comments. Different learning speeds should be respected.

4️⃣ Academic Integrity

No cheating, plagiarism, or unethical practices. Doubts should be discussed for understanding, not just answers.

5️⃣ Communication

Keep communication clear and relevant to studies. Avoid unnecessary distractions during study time. Honest feedback is encouraged.

6️⃣ Resource Sharing

Study materials, notes, and links may be shared only for educational purposes. Do not share personal or paid content without permission.

7️⃣ Privacy

Personal information, scores, or weaknesses shared during study sessions must be kept confidential.

8️⃣ Motivation & Support

Encourage each other during low-motivation periods. Healthy competition is allowed, comparison is not.

9️⃣ Conflict Resolution

Any misunderstandings should be discussed calmly. If issues persist, either member may choose to end the study mate arrangement respectfully.

🔟 Termination

Either study mate can exit the arrangement at any time with prior notice. No hard feelings—focus remains on personal growth.

✅ Acceptance

By becoming a study mate, both parties agree to follow these terms and maintain a positive learning environment.
